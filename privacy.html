Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 5, 2026

1. Overview

This policy explains how Project Odysseus collects, uses, and protects personal information for tutoring operations, session logging, approvals, payroll, and invoicing.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only what is needed to run tutoring and payroll workflows:

  • Tutors: name, email, phone, default hourly rate, active status.
  • Students: name, grade, guardian name/phone, notes, active status.
  • Assignments: subject, start/end dates, allowed days and time ranges.
  • Sessions: date, start/end time, duration, mode, location, notes, status, approvals.
  • Invoices: invoice number, period, totals, line items and amounts.
  • Audit logs: admin actions, timestamps, IP, and user agent.
  • Auth: magic link tokens (hashed) and session cookies.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Assign tutors to students and define session windows.
  • Log and approve tutoring sessions.
  • Generate weekly invoices and payroll exports.
  • Maintain audit trails for operational integrity.
  • Comply with legal and financial record-keeping duties.

4. Access and Roles

Admins can access all operational data. Tutors can access only their own assignments, sessions, payroll summaries, and invoices. Session approvals and payroll actions are admin-only.

5. Emails and Notifications

The system sends login links (magic links) by email. Invoice PDFs are generated on demand and are not stored or emailed by default.

6. Data Retention

We retain data for operational and legal purposes. Current defaults are:

  • Sessions and approvals: 7 years
  • Session history: 7 years
  • Invoices and invoice lines: 7 years
  • Audit logs: 5 years
  • Magic link tokens: 30 days
  • Privacy request records: 3 years

Retention windows can be adjusted for legal or operational needs.

7. Access, Correction, and Deletion Requests

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data. If deletion conflicts with required financial retention, we anonymize personal data while keeping totals and dates.

8. Cookies and Sessions

We use session cookies to keep you signed in and CSRF cookies to protect requests. Analytics cookies are optional and only load after opt-in via our cookie banner. Browser storage is used for consent and UX preferences (for example, theme and non-sensitive UI state).

9. Third Parties

  • Hosting: DigitalOcean (API and static site hosting).
  • CDN/Proxy: Cloudflare.
  • Email: an email delivery provider configured by the operator.
  • Forms: Formspree for contact form submissions.
  • Analytics: Google Analytics (optional, opt-in).
  • Advertising: Google AdSense scripts on arcade/public pages, subject to provider policy controls.

10. Security

Data is transmitted over HTTPS, access is role-based, and audit logs capture admin actions. We limit collection to the minimum required for operations.
