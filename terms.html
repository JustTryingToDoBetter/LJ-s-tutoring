1. Services

Project Odysseus provides private Mathematics tutoring services for students in Grades 8-12. We offer Pure Mathematics tutoring only and do not provide Mathematical Literacy tutoring. Sessions are conducted in-person at agreed locations or online via video conferencing platforms.

2. Booking & Scheduling

  • Sessions must be booked in advance via WhatsApp or our contact form.
  • Regular weekly slots are reserved on a first-come, first-served basis.
  • We tutor Monday to Thursday (5pm - 8pm) and limited weekend slots.
  • Session times will be confirmed via WhatsApp or email.
