1. Service Description

Project Odysseus provides tutoring services and a platform for managing assignments, session logging, approvals, invoices, and payroll exports.

2. Accounts and Access

  • Admins manage tutor/student records, approvals, and payroll.
  • Tutors can view only their own assignments, sessions, and invoices.
  • Login uses a magic link sent by email. Keep access links private.

3. Session Logging and Approvals

  • Tutors must log sessions accurately, including date, time, and duration.
  • Only submitted sessions can be approved or rejected by admins.
  • Approved sessions are used to generate weekly invoices.

4. Invoices and Payroll

  • Invoices are generated weekly for approved sessions.
  • Payroll CSV exports are available for approved weeks.
  • Adjustments may be applied by admins during open pay periods.

5. Data Accuracy

Users are responsible for providing accurate information. Incorrect logs or details may delay approvals or invoice generation.

6. Disputes

Disputes should be raised promptly. Admins can review session history and audit logs to resolve discrepancies.
